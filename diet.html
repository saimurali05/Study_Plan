Fiber Guide

Target: 35g fiber/day. Fiber keeps you full, slows digestion, feeds gut bacteria, and directly aids fat loss by stabilizing blood sugar.

🫘

Rajma (Kidney Beans)

8g fiber per 100g

Best fat-loss bean. Keeps you full for 4–5 hours. Eat boiled with lemon and spices — no rice needed.

🟡

Chickpeas (Chana)

10g fiber per 100g

King of legumes. As a chaat or salad base. High protein too. Perfect mid-morning snack.

🟠

Masoor Dal (Red Lentil)

8g fiber per 100g

Cheapest and fastest to cook. No rice, just dal soup. Daily digestive powerhouse.

🟢

Moong Dal

7g fiber per 100g

Lightest dal, easiest to digest. Perfect for dinner or evening soup. Low calorie, high nutrition.

🥬

Spinach (Palak)

2g fiber per 100g

Eat 100–150g daily in omelettes, soup, or stir-fry. Loaded with iron and magnesium too.

🥦

Broccoli

Steam or stir-fry. Excellent on Saturday veg day. Very low calorie, very filling volume.

🫚

Muesli (Plain)

5g fiber per 60g

Your daily breakfast base. Choose plain, no-sugar variety. Oats in muesli = slow carbs + fiber.

Guava

5g fiber per 100g

Highest fiber fruit available. Better than apple for fat loss. Eat as afternoon craving buster.

Habits to Avoid

These habits will sabotage your fat loss even if your diet is perfect. Cut them completely for the best results.

🍚

White Rice & Roti

Spikes insulin fast, signals body to store fat. Replace with dal, beans, or vegetables as your carb base.

Kills fat loss
🥤

Cold Drinks & Juice

Even 1 glass of cola = 150 kcal pure sugar. Packaged fruit juice is just sugar water. Switch to water or black coffee.

Liquid calories
🍞

Bread & Biscuits

White bread is white flour + sugar. Even 2 slices = 200 kcal of empty carbs. Biscuits = worst snack for fat loss.

Empty carbs
🍟

Fried Foods

Samosa, pakora, chips, fried chicken — triple the calories with zero nutrition. 1 samosa = 300+ kcal. Just avoid.

Calorie bomb
🌙

Eating After 9 PM

Night eating = calories stored as fat since activity is zero. Dinner by 8 PM max. Only water after that.

Fat storage
🛋️

Skipping Gym

Diet without gym = muscle loss, slow metabolism. You must gym 5 days/week minimum for this plan to work. No excuses.

Muscle loss
😴

Less Than 7 Hours Sleep

Sleep deprivation raises cortisol (fat-storage hormone) and increases hunger by 30%. Sleep is literally fat loss time.

Hormone disruption
🍫

Sweets & Desserts

Gulab jamun, ice cream, chocolate, cake — even small portions destroy a full day's deficit. Fruit is your only sweet.

Deficit breaker
☕

Coffee with Sugar/Milk

Adding sugar to coffee turns your fat burner into a fat maker. Black only. 2 cups max per day.

Wastes tool
📱

Eating While Scrolling

Distracted eating causes overeating by 30–40%. Always eat sitting down, focused on food. Your brain needs to register fullness.

Overeating
🧂

Too Much Salt

Excess sodium causes water retention — you'll look and feel bloated even when losing fat. Use spices instead for flavor.

Water retention
💧

Not Drinking Enough Water

Thirst is often mistaken for hunger. Drink 3–4L daily. Before every meal, drink 1 glass — reduces how much you eat.

False hunger
